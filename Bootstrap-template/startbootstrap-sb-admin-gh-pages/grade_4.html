Grade 3

Conditional Automation

Example: Audi A8 with Traffic Jam Pilot, Tesla Autopilot (3.0). The system can take full control in specific conditions (e.g., in traffic jams). The driver can be distracted but must be ready to intervene if prompted by the system.

Grade 4

High Automation

Example: Robotaxi prototypes (Waymo, Yandex, advanced Tesla FSD Beta versions). The vehicle can fully drive itself in specific geofenced areas and conditions without driver intervention. The driver can completely relax, but the system is limited to predefined scenarios.
